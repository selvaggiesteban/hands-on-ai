# Database Management

- Is the database schema normalized to at least 3NF where appropriate? [YES/NO]
- Are indexes created for frequently queried columns to optimize performance? [YES/NO]
- Is database access restricted to authorized applications and users only? [YES/NO]
- Are automated daily backups configured and verified regularly? [YES/NO]
- Is sensitive data encrypted at the column or table level? [YES/NO]
- Are slow queries monitored and analyzed for optimization opportunities? [YES/NO]
