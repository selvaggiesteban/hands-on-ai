# Issue Tracking

- Are issues created with a clear template? [YES/NO]
- Are labels used for categorization (bug, enhancement)? [YES/NO]
- Is priority and severity assigned? [YES/NO]
- Are reproduction steps included for bugs? [YES/NO]
- Is the assignee clear? [YES/NO]
- Are issues linked to PRs? [YES/NO]
