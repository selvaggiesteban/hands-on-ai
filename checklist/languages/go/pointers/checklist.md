# Pointers

- Are pointers used to share data or allow modification? [YES/NO]
- Are pointers avoided for small structs (pass by value is often faster)? [YES/NO]
- Is nil pointer dereference checked? [YES/NO]
- Are pointers to stack-allocated variables safe (escape analysis handles this)? [YES/NO]
- Is pointer arithmetic avoided (Go generally forbids it)? [YES/NO]
- Are receiver types (pointer vs value) chosen correctly for methods? [YES/NO]
