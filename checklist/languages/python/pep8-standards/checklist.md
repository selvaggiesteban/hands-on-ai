# PEP 8 Standards

- Is 4-space indentation used? [YES/NO]
- Are lines limited to 79 (or 88/100/120) characters? [YES/NO]
- Are imports sorted and grouped correctly? [YES/NO]
- Is whitespace used consistently around operators? [YES/NO]
- Are naming conventions followed (snake_case, PascalCase, UPPER_CASE)? [YES/NO]
- Is a linter (flake8, pylint) used to enforce PEP 8? [YES/NO]
