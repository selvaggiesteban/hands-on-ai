# Operational Excellence

- Are monitoring and alerting systems configured for all critical services? [YES/NO]
- Is there a defined Incident Response Plan? [YES/NO]
- Are backups automated and regularly tested for restoration? [YES/NO]
- Is capacity planning reviewed periodically? [YES/NO]
- Are operational runbooks documentation up-to-date? [YES/NO]
- Is there a post-incident review (post-mortem) process in place? [YES/NO]
