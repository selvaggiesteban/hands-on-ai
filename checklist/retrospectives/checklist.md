# Retrospectives

- Is the retrospective held regularly (end of sprint)? [YES/NO]
- Is the environment safe for open feedback? [YES/NO]
- Are 'Start, Stop, Continue' items identified? [YES/NO]
- Are action items assigned owners? [YES/NO]
- Is progress on previous action items reviewed? [YES/NO]
- Is the focus on process improvement, not blame? [YES/NO]
