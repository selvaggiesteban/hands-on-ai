# Root Cause Tracing

## Overview
Don't fix the symptom. Fix the cause.

## The 5 Whys
Ask why 5 times until you hit a process or logic flaw.

## Tracing Technique
1.  Isolate the behavior.
2.  Identify the exact line of failure.
3.  Trace data backwards.
