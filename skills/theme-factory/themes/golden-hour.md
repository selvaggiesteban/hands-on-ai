# Golden Hour

Captures the warmth and richness of the hour before sunset.

## Color Palette

- **Gold**: `#ffd700` - Focus elements
- **Amber**: `#ffc107` - Secondary actions
- **Warm Gray**: `#424242` - Contrast text
